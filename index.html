Terms of Attendance & Refund Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Refund Policy

Refunds are available if requested within 24 hours of your original purchase. After 24 hours, all sales are final and no refunds will be issued. To request a refund within the eligible window, please email auricmovement@outlook.com with your confirmation code and reason for cancellation.

2. Spot Transfers

After the 24-hour refund window has passed, you may transfer your spot to another person at no additional charge. All transfers must be completed at least 4 hours before your session start time — no transfers will be accepted after that point. The new attendee must complete a signed liability waiver before attending. Transfers can be initiated using the Transfer feature on this page with your confirmation code. Auric Movement reserves the right to deny a transfer at its discretion.

3. Arrival & Lateness Policy

Please plan to arrive at your session arrival time — 1:00 PM for Session 1 or 2:00 PM for Session 2. The first 5 minutes are reserved for a brief reformer orientation and review of standard Pilates moves and terminology. Class begins promptly at 1:05 PM / 2:05 PM.

There is a 5-minute grace period only. Any participant arriving after 1:05 PM (Session 1) or 2:05 PM (Session 2) will not be admitted. This policy is strictly enforced — we are operating within a booked shared space and must adhere to a firm schedule.

No refunds or transfers will be issued for late arrivals. Please factor in travel time, building access, and removing your shoes by the elevator before entering.

4. Event Cancellation or Postponement by Auric Movement

If Auric Movement cancels or postpones the event for any reason, all registered participants will receive a full refund. Refunds will be processed through the original payment method within 5–10 business days. Auric Movement is not responsible for any incidental costs incurred by participants such as travel or accommodation.

5. Code of Conduct

All attendees are expected to treat instructors, staff, and fellow participants with respect. Auric Movement is a welcoming, inclusive space. Any behavior deemed disruptive, disrespectful, or unsafe — including harassment of any kind — will result in removal from the session without refund.

6. Right to Refuse Entry Entry

Auric Movement reserves the right to refuse entry to any individual who: has not completed a signed liability waiver, arrives more than 10 minutes after the session start time, appears to be under the influence of alcohol or substances, or poses a safety risk to themselves or others. No refund will be issued in these circumstances.

7. Photography & Video

Photography and video may be captured during the event for promotional purposes. A notice will be posted at the venue entrance. If you do not wish to be photographed, please notify your instructor before the session begins or submit a written opt-out request to auricmovement@outlook.com.

8. Medical Disclaimer

Pilates and movement-based activities involve physical exertion and carry inherent risk. Participants are strongly encouraged to consult a physician before attending if they have any medical conditions, injuries, or health concerns. Auric Movement instructors are not licensed medical professionals. Participation is at your own risk.

9. Contact

For any questions about these terms, please contact us at auricmovement@outlook.com.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026 · Last updated: May 2026

Auric Movement ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the New York SHIELD Act, the federal CAN-SPAM Act, and other applicable laws. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you register for our events.

1. Information We Collect

We collect your full legal name and email address when you register for an event or join a waitlist. We also record the date and time of your signed waiver and the session you selected.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to: (a) confirm your registration; (b) send event communications including session reminders and access instructions; (c) notify waitlisted participants when a spot becomes available; (d) maintain a record of signed liability waivers; and (e) process cancellations.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Registration data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and transmitted via EmailJS to our inbox at auricmovement@outlook.com. We do not operate a centralized database. Data is not transferred outside the United States.

4. Third-Party Payment Processing

Payment processing for our events is handled by a third-party payment provider. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment card details. Any financial information you provide during checkout is submitted directly to and governed by the privacy policy and terms of that third-party processor. We encourage you to review their privacy policy before completing any transaction.

5. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Email Communications

By registering, you consent to receive transactional emails related to your registration (confirmation, cancellation, waitlist updates). These are not marketing emails. Each email includes our contact information as required by the CAN-SPAM Act.

7. Your Rights (NY SHIELD Act)

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To make a request, email us at auricmovement@outlook.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information, consistent with the requirements of the New York SHIELD Act.

9. Minors

Our events and this website are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.
