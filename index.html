Terms of Attendance & Refund Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Refund Policy

Refunds are available if requested within 24 hours of your original purchase. After 24 hours, all sales are final. To request a refund or a spot transfer to a friend, email auricmovement@outlook.com with your name, session, and reason.

2. Cancellations & Transfers

To cancel your spot or transfer it to a friend, please email auricmovement@outlook.com directly. Include your full name, session, and the name and email of the person taking your spot if transferring. All requests must be received at least 4 hours before your session start time. Auric Movement reserves the right to deny a transfer at its discretion.

3. Arrival & Lateness Policy

Please arrive at your session arrival time — 1:00 PM for Session 1 or 2:00 PM for Session 2. The first 5 minutes are reserved for a reformer orientation. Class begins promptly at 1:05 PM / 2:05 PM.

There is a 5-minute grace period only. Any participant arriving after 1:05 PM (Session 1) or 2:05 PM (Session 2) will not be admitted. This is strictly enforced. No refunds or transfers will be issued for late arrivals.

4. Event Cancellation by Auric Movement

If Auric Movement cancels or postpones the event, all registered participants will receive a full refund within 5–10 business days via the original payment method. Auric Movement is not responsible for incidental costs such as travel or accommodation.

5. Code of Conduct

All attendees are expected to treat instructors, staff, and fellow participants with respect. Auric Movement is a welcoming, inclusive space. Disruptive, disrespectful, or unsafe behavior will result in removal from the session without refund.

6. Right to Refuse Entry

Auric Movement reserves the right to refuse entry to any individual who has not completed a signed liability waiver, arrives late, appears to be under the influence of alcohol or substances, or poses a safety risk. No refund will be issued in these circumstances.

7. Photography & Video

Photography and video may be captured during the event for promotional purposes. A notice will be posted at the venue. To opt out, notify your instructor before the session or email auricmovement@outlook.com.

8. Medical Disclaimer

Pilates involves physical exertion and inherent risk. Please consult a physician before attending if you have any medical conditions or health concerns. Auric Movement instructors are not licensed medical professionals. Participation is at your own risk.

9. Third-Party Payment

Payment processing is handled by a third-party provider. We do not store your payment details. Refer to your payment provider's privacy policy for information on how your financial data is handled.

Effective Date: May 2026 · Last updated: May 2026

Auric Movement ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the New York SHIELD Act, the federal CAN-SPAM Act, and other applicable laws.

1. Information We Collect

We collect your full legal name and email address when you register for an event or join a waitlist. We also record the date and time of your signed waiver and the session you selected.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to: (a) confirm your registration; (b) send event communications; (c) notify waitlisted participants when a spot opens; (d) maintain a record of signed liability waivers; and (e) process cancellations.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Registration data is stored via Google Sheets (a Google LLC service) and transmitted via EmailJS to our inbox at auricmovement@outlook.com. We do not operate a centralized proprietary database. Data is not transferred outside the United States. All registration data will be permanently deleted within 30 days following the event date.

Payment processing is handled by a third-party payment provider. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment card details. Any financial information you provide is submitted directly to and governed by the privacy policy of that third-party processor.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Email Communications

By registering, you consent to receive transactional emails related to your registration. Each email includes our contact information as required by the CAN-SPAM Act.

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. Email us at auricmovement@outlook.com. We will respond within 30 days.
